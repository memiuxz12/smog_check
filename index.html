FAQ

Common questions asked by customers

What is a smog check?

Smog checks is process designed to identify vehicles that are polluting the environment so they can be properly repaired. The smog check program is to help improve the air we breathe.

What do I need for my smog check?

If your vehicle is subject to a smog check, remember to take the notification from the DMV with you. This will help sure that the correct vehicle information is sent to the DMV.

How can I help my vehicle pass the Smog Check?

Keeping your vehicle well maintained and not tampering the emission related components will help improve your vehicleâ€™s chances of passing the smog check.

How often does my vehicle need to get a smog check?

Your vehicle MUST get a smog check every two years. A smog check is also required if you sell your vehicle and if its more than four years old.

What happens if my vehicle fails the smog check inspection?

The technician will explain the reason for failure. You will need to obtain the repairs necessary for it to pass the smog check next time.
